Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you provide?

We provide website design and development, technical consulting, and setup/configuration services for small businesses and personal projects.

How do I get started?

You can reach out with details about your project. From there, we'll review your requirements and provide a quote along with next steps.

How are paid services or projects priced?

Paid services or projects are priced based on scope, complexity, and time required. A clear quote will be provided before any work begins.

Do you require payment upfront?

Payment terms are discussed and agreed upon before each project begins. For smaller projects, payment may be completed upon delivery, while larger or ongoing work may be split into stages.

Do you offer refunds?

Due to the nature of custom development and consulting work, refunds are generally not provided once work has begun.

However, reasonable revisions are included within the agreed project scope to ensure the final result aligns with your expectations.

If a project is paused or canceled early, partial refunds may be considered depending on the amount of work already completed.

How long does a project take?

Timelines depend on the scope of the project. Estimated timelines will be provided during the proposal stage.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes, reasonable revisions are included based on the project scope. Additional revisions outside the agreed scope may incur extra charges where paid services or projects are concerned.

Will you provide ongoing support?

Ongoing support or maintenance can be arranged if needed, depending on the project.

What do you need from me to begin?

Typically, project details, content (text/images), and any specific requirements or preferences you may have.

Can you help with hosting or domain setup?

Yes, we can assist with setup and configuration as part of the project or as a separate service.
